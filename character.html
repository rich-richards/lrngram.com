Meet Learn

Learn is a 3-year-old boy with a slightly rounded, squared blue head and body. He has blue eyes (blue iris, black pupil), a nose, a constant big smile, and distinctive L-shaped ears that slightly extend over his head. He wears a white t-shirt, blue dungarees, and white sneakers.

Learn's mission is to explore the reality and truth of the universe through fun and engaging adventures. Join him on his journey to learn English in the most magical way!
