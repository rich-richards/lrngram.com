How it works

  1. Validation: single-word alphabetic verbs only. The app checks the dictionary to confirm it's a verb.
  2. Converts verb to useful search forms: base, -ing, past (irregular if known), "verb action".
  3. Searches Wikimedia Commons for video first (best to visualize action), then images and audio. If no audio found, browser TTS speaks an example.
