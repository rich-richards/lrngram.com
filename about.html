Our Story

Learngram was created by Mekulu Awolo Thierry Edouard in 2024. It is a platform designed for children and people who have no basic knowledge of the English language.

English is the foundation language on Learngram. Through interactive lessons, games, and a joyful approach, Learngram provides all the fun and engagement children and beginners need to learn efficiently.

Making learning fun, one game at a time!
