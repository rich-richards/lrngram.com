About the Series

Our children's book series teaches English fundamentals through inquisitive, funny, engaging, and interesting stories. Each book follows Learn, a curious 3-year-old boy, as he explores the universe and discovers new words, concepts, and adventures.

The stories are designed to make learning English fun and magical, helping young children build a strong foundation in vocabulary and sentence structure while sparking their imagination.

Created by Mekulu Awolo Thierry Edouard in 2024, Learngram brings joy and education together for the next generation of English learners.
